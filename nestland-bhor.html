About the Project

Nestled in the scenic hills of Bhor, just 40 km from Katraj, Pune, this project offers breathtaking dam backwater views and a serene natural setting. With tar road access, electricity connection, and water supply, you can enjoy modern conveniences while surrounded by nature. The gated community ensures safety and privacy, making it perfect for a weekend farmhouse, organic plantation, or long-term investment.
